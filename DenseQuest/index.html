Unsupervised DenseQuest

Selection and Ranking

Simply upload you dataset and find out which model is the best for you.

DenseQuest offers unsupervised selection and ranking features to identify the most suitable pre-trained dense retriever from a pool, customized for a provided target collection.

To request access to the live system, please contact us at e.khramtsova@uq.edu.au.

The live system can be accessed at the scheduled time via live-densequest.ielab.io.
